---
title: What's a good score?
---
There's no universal "passing" score — it depends on the role and how competitive the applicant pool is. As a general guide:

- **75%+** — Strong match. Apply with confidence.
- **60-74%** — Worth applying, but tailor your resume first.
- **Below 60%** — Significant gaps. Focus on adding missing keywords you genuinely have experience with.
